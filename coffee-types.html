☕ Popular Vietnamese Coffee Types

Discover the diverse world of Vietnamese coffee drinks, from traditional classics to modern innovations. Each type offers a unique taste experience that reflects Vietnam's rich coffee culture.

Cà Phê Sữa Đá

The most iconic Vietnamese coffee - strong coffee over ice with sweet condensed milk. Perfect for hot days!

Cà Phê Đen

Pure black coffee served hot or iced. For those who love the bold, robust flavor of Vietnamese coffee beans.

Cà Phê Trứng

Hanoi's specialty - coffee topped with creamy egg yolk foam. Sweet, rich, and surprisingly delicious!

Cà Phê Sữa Chua

A unique combination of coffee and yogurt. The tangy yogurt balances the bitter coffee perfectly.

Cà Phê Cốt Dừa

Coffee with coconut milk - a tropical twist on the traditional recipe. Creamy and refreshing!

Bạc Xỉu

The "reverse" coffee - more milk than coffee. Mild and sweet, perfect for those who prefer less caffeine.
